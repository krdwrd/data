<p align="justify"><font face="Georgia"><kw class="krdwrd-tag-3">Besides fighting, drinking and
plundering, pirates are known for making music!!!!&nbsp; Every pirate loves to
sing (though, you may not love to hear him!)&nbsp; But there are some musical
pirates that are a delight to listen to.&nbsp; The Corsairs gave us many hours
of entertainment at the Pirates in Paradise Festival last year, and we were glad
to see they made it again this year.&nbsp; Besides the Corsairs, we were thrilled the Pyrates Royale made it this time, as well
as the lads &amp; lasses from the NY Ren Faire, who call themselves the Crimson
Pirates. Other groups sang as well, including members of On the Lamb and the
Pyrates of Tortuga.&nbsp; But a pirate doesn't have to belong to an official
musical troupe in order to entertain himself and others with song.&nbsp; In fact
... just try and stop him (or her).</kw></font></p>
