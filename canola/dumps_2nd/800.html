Hyperbolic Marco
Description
Hyperbolic Marco is one of those Hyperbolics that always seems to be looking at you... it can be kind of creepy. He's harmless though, partially due to the fact that he's a piece of crochet work and cannot move. He's a little smaller than his other Hyperbolic "cousins"... except for Al. He adds a little personality to wherever you place him. He's safe for little kids, as there's nothing for them to choke on and great for the adults who are kids at heart.

Hyperbolic Marco is crocheted out of red and blue acrylic yarn and rainbow crochet cotton. His eyeball is crocheted out of cotton and then sewn on after the crochet part was finished. He's about 5 inches (12.7 cm) wide and roughly 3 inches (7.6 cm) tall.

The bottom or math portion of Hyperbolic Marco is done with the idea of a hyperbolic plane.
