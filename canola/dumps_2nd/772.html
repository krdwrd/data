Part 2: The Voyage

Let's get started. While on this adventure you will create a "Wanted Poster" for a pirate you learned about on your voyage.  The poster will include a clip art picture of the pirate you choose, with a listing of his/her crimes and any reward that will be offered.

You will use Microsoft Publisher software to make and print the poster.

To spy on the pirate you choose, follow the steps below to be successful. But be careful that the pirate doesn't capture YOU!  While spying on your pirate you need to think about these questions:

1. What is he/she is famous for? 

2. What type of crimes did the pirate commit during his/her lifetime? 

The "Wanted Poster" must be your design, so be as creative as you want to be. When the posters are finished they will be displayed in the hallway for other classes to see.
