COMMISSIONED WORK

 

Marco is the HOTTEST gun for hire in the POP Art game! Featured above is a detail of the the interior of a poolhouse on an Estate in the Hamptons that he adorned,for more pics of it and a 24hr Deli click HERE plus for a fab pic of the whole poolhouse grab your towel & click HERE

Marco's very first exposure to art was 2nd Grade art class, it was his favorite time of the day when he could get loose with his friends and turn big blank sheets of white paper into war-zones where the army men battled with each other, where clunky tanks tumbled down hillsides into mine-fields and impossible planes soared through the crooked clouds!

Not too much has changed, Marco skipped Art school and every project still begins with that same sheet of blank white paper that gets translated onto a sketch on canvas which becomes a painting.

Marco has created thousands of paintings for a dazzling array of clients such as the skate deck he just did for ESPN's X Games!  

Marco, a long time board member and one of the pioneering artists in the Lower East Side came out slinging paint when he was comissioned by the Lower East Side Business Improvement District to paint a shopping a drinking (a dangerous combination) themed piece on four panels, two of which would be auctioned off while the remaining two would be given to the Lower East Side B.I.D. and Metro Newspaper at....

TIME FOR A WELL EARNED REST BEFORE THE NEXT PROJECT...
"NO PLANE NO GAME" is a design that Marco Created as a Limited Edition Print for Marquis Jet's Super Bowl 2008 Party in Paradise Valley, Arizona.  
 
Marco, who is the CAO or Chief Art Officer for Marquis Jet, was on hand to handle the merchandise and sign the prints which were printed in three sizes on stretched canvas. Over 900 people attended the event on Saturday February 2nd, 2008 which was held at a plush desert ranch villa. Steve Tish, Giants Owner, New England Patriots head coach Bill Belichick, ESPN's Chris Berman and many many more luminaries and tastemakers from the worlds of business, sports, and entertainment were present on a picture perfect sunny Saturday in Arizona to enjoy all the festivites a day before they were all surpised, some happily, some not so happily, with the NY Giants stunning upset of the highly favored New England Patriots the following dayThe name of the design "NO PLANE NO GAME" is a referance to the number of players and other vips which Marquis flew down to Scottsdale for the Big Game.

A lot of Marco's comissions are for Non Profit Organizations-Let's say you work with or know someone or know someone who knows someone who works with a charity and would like to comission Marco to create a custom piece for the annual gala....no problem! Here's a piece he did for Homes For the Homeless.

 

Or let's say you wholesale Canary diamonds and want Marco to create something just for you...consider it DONE!

Or maybe it's you're 10th Anniversary and you want something more special and unique than Jewlrey, something that's uniquely yours...

Or maybe your an old school LES(Lower East Side) resident who remembers when Williamsburg was a place where a lot of Orthodox Jewish people lived and you lived in the Lower East Side with Marcoart and Max Fish, Amy Downs, and Mary Adams, Clayton Outlaw Art Gallery, and Alleged Art Gallery, TG-170, the original Triple 5 Soul, and ABC No Rio, and you remember when A Life, and Tim Toum,  Cherry, the Orchard Bar, and Kush first opened because you were there before they were there, and the owners were your friends and now most of them are gone or have moved and these big high rises are going up where you used to go to outlaw parties where the djs spun vinyl, a "suggested donation" got you as many plastic cups of the house punch as you could handle, the smoke was so thick you couldn't see who you were talking to and it didn't matter because the music was so good, the vibe was just right, nobody was texting anybody on their cell phones, and everyone was getting down.

Now you're fed up with all the poseurs who are a day late and a dollar long. So you commission Marco to create a piece which details the end of the glory days so those who don't may now know that American Apparel, Starbucks, and Boutique Hotels headed down to the hood to crash and cash in on a party that no one invited them to and the joke was on them because it was over before they ever opened their doors, and they could care less, they've got bigger fish(read: neighborhoods) to fry: in half the time it took the pioneering Predecessors of the now ousted hipsters to put the LES on the map these ultra-cool developers will make the Lower East Side as cool as the Upper East Side. 

So if you have a thought, concept or idea that you'd like Marco to express visually in his own inimicable style, rest assured that he's is on the case like can of Mace, splashing colors all over the blank face of a canvas until it's miles of tecnicolor smiles

Artistically speaking, what it all really boils down to is not what Marco can do for you, but what Marco can’t do for you, “not much!” would be an appropriate response. Aside from the paintings and prints that are currently featured in the web gallery, Marco can custom paint practically anything you like on virtually any surface imaginable, use the categories below as a very basic guide to what’s possible.  If you don’t see it below, please request it, Marco always does his best to meet every creative challenge and fulfill or exceed your expectations!”

To say Marco's client list is impressive is an understatement-he's done work for some of the biggest athletes, celebrities, influencers, tastemakers and scenesters in the buisneess...so you're in good company...there's a piece for every profession...let's say you run a VERY successful chain of Gentleman's Clubs and would like something for your office...look no further! Marco has just the thing!

Marco makes his own stretchers and stretches his own canvas which means he can make you ANY size canvas you want even a 39" by 43 and ½" piece to fit between your sofa and your bed, or a 27"and 3/4" by 67" and ¼ “ piece to fit between the sconces in the entryway. Use this section in conjunction with the image database to come up with a fabulous custom-themed piece that’s right for you. There's no need to be shy, no request is too bizarre, Marco has painted people's pets because their pet's name was Marco, he's done witty word plays for plastic surgeons, and plant doctors, he's created thousands of paintings and shows no sign of slowing down. 

But can I commission Marco to do a POP Portriat for me, my friends, clients, family??? YES!!! Marco does SO many Portraits that they have thier own fabulous section which you MUST visit by clicking HERE!

The soul-satisfaction that he experiences when he creates a piece is a exhilarating rush that surpasses any drug and Marco is truly addicted to creating custom paintings for all types of demanding clients or producing whimsical scenes that feature his colorful icons and kinetic scenes.

Marco's paintings and limited edition prints are available worldwide through a network of galleries that love his work as much as he does. His work circles the globe and he is often following it from America to Europe to the Far East, amazed that it all launched from a street corner in SOHO.

Marco has painted canvases on the streets of Taiwan,

 

in a nightclub in Amsterdam-click HERE to see pics, in a department store in Tokyo, in an office in Switzerland, in a Warehouse in Germany, For the Athens Summer Olympic Games-click HERE to see pic-in a mall in LA...he's done portraits of dogs, painted Smart cars, fiberglass cows, champagne bottles, gates, people, and many, many canvases.

Marco loves to be commissioned to custom create something according to a theme a charming young man by the name of Johnny Korn recently had a brainstorming session with Marco to come up with some cute word-plays on his name with predictably Korny results!

So what are you waiting for? Marco to die? By then it'll be to late, his estate will have all the loot and you'll be fresh out of luck and a too cool for school POP Art Portrtiat by the Artist curently known as Marco!
