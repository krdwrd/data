The first time it had been an accident. They were on a routine mission, searching for a new mutant whose family hadn’t been able to handle her natural bright pink hair and purple eyes. She had the power to wake the dead, literally. The professor sent Logan and Jean to get her, believing they would be best for the job.

Scott and Rogue hadn’t liked it, but they didn’t complain. They trusted their lovers to be faithful, and the professor knew what he was doing. But, as these things usually go, Scott and Rogue had been right not to trust their significant others alone. Logan and Jean had only been in Michigan for ten minutes before the close proximity in the X-van was too much. By the time they’d finished, Elly – the mutant – had been taken by Magneto.

Professor Xavier wasn’t too happy about this, but he was much too worried about Magneto’s future plans to really lecture them on not paying close enough attention. Besides, the guilty looks they kept shooting each other were enough to tell him they understood the repercussions of their actions. Surely, they would think twice before doing it again, right?

Wrong.
